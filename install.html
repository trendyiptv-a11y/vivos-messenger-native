Install VIVOS Messenger

VIVOS Messenger is currently distributed through Google Play testing.

Testing access may be required

How to get access:

1. Tap Request tester access and send your Gmail address.

2. Wait until your Gmail address is approved as a tester.

3. Open the Google Play link below.

4. Accept the test and install VIVOS Messenger from Google Play.

Note: If the Play link does not open, your Gmail address may not have been added as tester yet.

Request tester access Open Google Play
